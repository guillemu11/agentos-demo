## Design Context

### Users
VP and Directors of Marketing at enterprise companies (Emirates as primary client). They view the platform in office settings during business hours, often presenting to stakeholders. Need to quickly understand value proposition and platform capabilities.

### Brand Personality
Decisive, intelligent, orchestrated. The interface should feel like a command center — controlled confidence, not flashy tech.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Dark theme (matches existing AgentOS dashboard)
- Utilitarian/refined — closer to Linear or Vercel than to Stripe or Apple
- Anti-references: generic SaaS landing pages, gradient-heavy AI marketing sites, cards-with-icons grids
- Must use CSS custom properties (no Tailwind), Lucide React icons (no emojis)
- Existing color palette via CSS variables in index.css :root

### Design Principles
1. Narrative over feature-listing — every section tells part of a story
2. Hierarchy through spacing and typography, not decoration
3. Professional restraint — no gratuitous effects, every visual element earns its place
4. Data speaks — before/after comparisons, concrete workflows, not vague promises
5. Scannable by executives — clear headings, short text blocks, visual anchors
